Want to learn more about Celiac Disease and Gluten Sensitivity? You're in the right place.

What is Celiac Disease?

Celiac Disease is a condition in which a person's small intestine becomes hypersensitive to gluten. This usually leads to difficulty in digesting food. The YouTube channel YouBeauty has an informational video that shows what happens when gluten enters the body of a person with Celiac Disease.

What is Gluten Sensitivity?

Gluten Sensitivity is a condition that appears to be a more mild version of Celiac Disease. A person with Gluten Sensitivity might experience a variety of symptoms that appear to be greatly lessened when put on a gluten-free diet. The YouTube channel Dr. Josh Axe published a video describing some of the symptoms and side effects of Gluten Sensitivity.
