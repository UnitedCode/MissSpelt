About Us



When we started MissSpelt! In 2015, we had one goal in mind. We wanted to make great food, served gluten free. Now more than a year later with 4 locations, 100+ staff, and thousands served, we think that we are well on our way to achieving our goal.



We pride ourselves with the level of service and quality we are able to achieve at all of our locations. From the freshness of our food to the atmosphere of our restaurants, rest assured that we pay attention to every detail to make sure that you have the best experience possible with your friends and family.

Meet the Team


Jonathon Rogers

Founder

I was diagnosed with gluten sensitivity in November of 2001. This always made going out to eat with friends and family a major hassle. That’s why I decided to start MissSpelt! To have a place that anyone can come to and eat great food, gluten free.

Brianna Thompson

Co-Founder

I’ve felt much healthier by cutting gluten out of my diet. Ever since then I’ve promoted cooking gluten free foods. So when Jonathon came to me with the idea of MissSpelt! I felt that it was a fantastic opportunity to make the world a happier, healthier place.

Frequently Asked Questions



Do you have a loyalty program?

We sure do! If you are a regular at any of our locations, you can register for our loyalty program here.

Why did you start MissSpelt?

We started MissSpelt because we felt that there just weren’t any good restaurants that catered to gluten-free eaters, and we felt that there should be. And so MissSpelt! was born.


Do you offer any meals for vegans?

Yes! Our menu has multiple items that contain no meat and some of these that contain no eggs either.

I have a large group, is there a way to make sure there will be seating for everyone?

Absolutely! You can make a reservation for any of our locations by filling out this online form

Do you have Wi-Fi and power outlets available to your customers?

Most Definitely! Whether it be for work or relaxation, you are welcome to use our power outlets and our public Wi-Fi to power your electronic devices.
